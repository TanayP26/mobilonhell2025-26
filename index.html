WHAT IS MOBILON?

Mobilon is the official App Development Club of Bennett University, dedicated to fostering innovation and expertise in mobile application development.

We are a community of passionate developers focused on creating high-quality, cross-platform applications. Our mission is to empower students to transform their ideas into functional, beautiful, and market-ready apps.

While we have a strong focus on Flutter for robust cross-platform development, we do not exclusively work on Flutter! Our expertise spans a wide range of cutting-edge mobile technologies, including Java, Kotlin, Swift, and Jetpack Compose.

Mobilon Events

MID TERM MOCHA

Mid Term Mocha is a special mentorship initiative organized by Mobilon, specifically designed to help first-year students prepare thoroughly for their mid-term examinations.

In this focused teaching and review session, senior members of Mobilon guide their juniors through critical core subjects, ensuring they can approach their exams with confidence and strong foundational knowledge.

MOBILE NEXT

At Bennett University, Mobilon proudly hosted an exciting project showcase where delegates delivered inspiring speeches on app development. Enthusiastic attendees explored vibrant stalls, discovering innovative student projects that highlight the future of technology.

ROUND TABLE CONFERENCE

Mobilon's Round Table Conference (RTC) was an inter-club event designed to spark students' interest in developing innovative mobile app ideas. It was a concept-based event where participants presented new and unique app ideas that have the potential to be implemented in the future.

FLUTTER.AWAY 2.0

Mobilon’s flutter.away 2.0 was a hit, igniting the passion for app technologies among junior students! The star of the show was the interactive challenge where teams successfully designed and developed a practical 'formula app.' This event was packed with fun, focused learning, and hands-on creation, proving a huge success in launching the next generation of talented app developers
